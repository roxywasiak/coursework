Bio

Grimes is a successful musician from Cananda with a large following of her own. Her eleectronic pop music showcases her versatility as an artist. She writes, produces and edits her own albums. Grimes is also a visual artist who captivates her 1.7 million Instagram followers with illustrations and graphics she creates herself. Her work unveils her punk , goth and e-girl aesthetic. Her eecentric style and personality makes her somewhat of a bewilderment which keeps her fans enamoured.
